CHRONARIUM PRO TIPS

This is the planning chamber of SomniaScapes: a quiet place to lay out days, tasks, anchors, and routines without letting them become a storm.

  • The Planner: Open the planner to place events, tasks, and anchors into day, week, or month view.
  • Event Forge: Choose a day, add one anchor, and let that be enough to begin.
  • Chronarium XP: Planning actions add room XP. The first planner entry unlocks Tick, the calendar moth.
  • Presets: The preset vault changes the planning atmosphere. Tiny calendar games live there too.
  • The Keeper: The avatar is a lore guide for the room, not a chat box. It keeps the planning chamber feeling alive.
  • Tick: Tick appears after your first planner entry and patrols the room like a tiny calendar familiar.
  • Lore: The Chronarium gathers scattered days into lantern-lit order. It is a place for one next step, not the whole future.
  • Cloak UI: Hide the panels when the room is set and you want only the clock, weather, ambience, and calm.
