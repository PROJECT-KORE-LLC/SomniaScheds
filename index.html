SOMNIASCHEDS PRO TIPS

Welcome to your highly aesthetic, offline-first digital scheduler. Here is how to make the most of it:

  • The Planner: Click "Planner" in the top navigation to enter The Atrium. Your schedule and tasks are stored safely and permanently inside your device via IndexedDB.
  • Sensory Control: Use the "Atmos" tab to adjust the speed, brightness, and opacity of your environment to down-regulate sensory overhead during long planning sessions.
  • Vibe Architecture: Mix and match up to 60 total aesthetic variables. Try "Gothic" + "Bats" + "Inferno" for a dark vibe, or "Library" + "Motes" + "Sepia" for deep focus!
  • Cloak UI: Once your vibe is set, hit "Cloak UI" to hide all menus so you can focus entirely on the atmosphere and widgets. Click anywhere on the screen to bring the menus back.

💾 Backup & Cloud Sync (Local-First Approach)

Because your data never leaves your device automatically, you are entirely in control of your backups:
1. Export: Use the "Export Vibes" (Vault tab) and "Export Planner" (Planner side panel) buttons to download your environment settings and schedules as .json files.
2. Secure: Upload these files into Google Drive, a secure local folder, or external media for safekeeping.
3. Restore: If you clear your browser data or switch devices, simply use the "Import" buttons to inject your exact architectural setup and schedule back into the engine.

🤖 Pro Tip: Awakening the AI Assistant

To utilize natural language scheduling (e.g. "Schedule a 2-hour deep work block for KORE architecture tomorrow"):
1. Go to aistudio.google.com and sign in with your Google account.
2. Click "Get API key" in the left sidebar and create a key.
3. Open the Planner from the top navigation in SomniaScheds.
4. Paste it into the "Gemini API Key" field at the top of the AI Assistant panel and click Save Key.
Privacy Note: Your key and prompts never leave your local workspace except at the exact moment of parsing.
