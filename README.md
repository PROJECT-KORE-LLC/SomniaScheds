# 🌙 SOMNIASCHEDS 
**Atrium of the Somniaverse**

This district of the Somniaverse is still forming

**Less bloat. More focus. Absolute sovereignty.**

Modern productivity software is broken. It is bloated with social integrations, notification badges, cloud-syncing delays, and predatory subscription models. **SomniaScheds** is the antidote.

This is a local-first, zero-dependency environment designed to act as your private digital workshop. It fuses a robust ambient audio/visual engine with a minimalist day planner, habit tracker, and private journal. 

No servers. No tracking. No distractions. Just pure, unadulterated focus.

---

## 🔮 The Core Pillars

### 1. Absolute Data Sovereignty
Your data belongs to you. SomniaScheds uses your browser's native `IndexedDB` and `localStorage` to securely house your media, journal entries, and schedules strictly on your hard drive. There is no login screen, and no corporate server holding your thoughts hostage. Export your entire Sanctuary as a JSON backup at any time.

### 2. The Ambient Focus Engine
The environment you work in dictates the quality of your work.
*   **Web Audio Mixer:** Blend 10+ custom audio tracks (rain, fire, ticking clocks) with master gain controls.
*   **Visual Palettes:** Shift the entire interface from `Lunar Mode` to `Noir Mode` with a single click.
*   **Particle Systems:** Add subtle, non-distracting motion (like floating dust or falling embers) to keep your screen feeling "alive" without breaking your concentration.

### 3. The Minimalist Planner & Scribes
*   **Daily Tallies:** Track Pomodoros, water intake, or reps with a clean `+/-` tally system.
*   **Focus Timer:** A built-in timer that doesn't just flash when it ends—it triggers a gentle, synthesized audio chime through the Web Audio API to pull you out of your deep work peacefully.
*   **SomniaScribes (The Codex):** A private, beautifully formatted journal. Log timestamped entries into an archive that feels like an ancient, digital grimoire.

## 🚀 How to Enter the Sanctuary

You do not need to install anything, configure a database, or download dependencies. 

1. Download the `index.html` (or `app.html`) file.
2. Double-click it to open it in your browser.
3. Breathe.

*(Pro-Tip: Press `F11` or use the built-in Fullscreen button to completely hide your browser UI and immerse yourself in the environment).*

---

## 🛠️ The Architecture

Built for resilience and longevity, SomniaScheds requires no external libraries to function at its core. 
*   **HTML5 / CSS3 / Vanilla JS** (Zero framework bloat)
*   **IndexedDB** for high-capacity local media storage (Upload massive background images without crashing your browser storage limits).
*   **Web Audio API** for synthesized chimes and complex audio mixing.
